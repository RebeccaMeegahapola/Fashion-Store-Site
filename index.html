Our Journey

Our story began from our Founder's discovery of a gap in the market for well-made Workwear, for the new working woman, at a reasonable cost. In 2012, Fashion was born, and our Workwear Collection slowly made its way to clothing stores across Colombo. With our brand’s popularity increasing, so did the need for our very own store. Today, the Fashion range includes our Casual & Linen Collection, Workwear Collection & Kids Collections.
